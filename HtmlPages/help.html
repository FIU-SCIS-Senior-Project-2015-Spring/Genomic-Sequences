GenomePro Help Center!

Creating An Account


Select the Register Blue Button
  • Enter your First Name
  • Enter your Last Name
  • Enter your email address
  • Enter your password
  • Confirm your password
  • Press the Register Blue Button!

If your ready you can clik Here! Register.


Log In


Select the Log In Blue Button
  • Enter your email address
  • Enter your password
  • Press the Log In Blue Button!

If your ready you can clik Here! Log In.


Contact Us


Select In the Menu Bar The Contact Us Tab
  • Enter your Full Name
  • Enter your email address
  • Enter your subject
  • Enter your message
  • Press the Send Blue Button!

If your ready you can clik Here! Contact Us.


Forgot Password


Select Forgot your Password Blue Link
  • Enter your email address
  • Press Recover Button!

If your ready you can clik Here! Forgot Password.


Change Password


Select Chane Password On Profile
  • Enter your current password
  • Enter your new password
  • Enter to confirm your new password
  • Press the Change Button!

How To Use Current Applications Once You Are Login

Find Genome Data Type
  • Press Tools Tab On Menu Bar
  • Upload One Genome File

Find Genome Probes
  • Press Tools Tab On Menu Bar
  • Upload One Genome File And One Bash File
  • Press Find Button!
Find Genome Differences
  • Press Tools Tab On Menu Bar

  • Press Find Button!
